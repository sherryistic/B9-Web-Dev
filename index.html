Ingredients:

  1. Oyster sauce 1 tbs
  2. Soy sauce 2 tb
  3. Sesame oil 1 tsp
  4. Cheeni (Sugar) 1 tbs (optional)
  5. Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
  6. Safed mirch powder (White pepper powder) ½ tsp
  7. Corn flour 2 tbs
  8. Chicken boneless cut in strips 250 gms
  9. Chow mein sauce 1 tbs
  10. Oil 2 tbs
  11. Lehsan (Garlic) finely chopped 1 tbs
  12. Gajar (Carrot) julienne ½ Cup
  13. Bund gobhi (Cabbage) shredded 2 Cups
  14. Shimla mirch (Capsicum) julienne ½ Cup
  15. Hara pyaz ka safed hissa (Green onion white part) julienne ¼ Cup
  16. Sirka (Vinegar) 2 tbs
  17. Chow mein or Egg noodles (Boiled) 1 & ½ Cups
  18. Yakhni (Chicken stock) ¼ Cup
  19. Namak (Salt) ½ tsp or to taste
  20. Hara pyaz (Green onion) julienne ½ Cup
  21. Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ½ tsp
